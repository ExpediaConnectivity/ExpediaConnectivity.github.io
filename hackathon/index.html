Winners and Prizes

1st:   200,000 Expedia+ Points: Awarded to HotelOga for their solution to facilitate property acquisitions in emerging markets

2nd:  100,000 Expedia+ Points: Awarded to HotelSpider for their solution that also facilitates property acquisitions by crowdsourcing content and making onboarding to Expedia a very simple process

3rd:   50,000 Expedia+ Points: Awarded to a team of 3 different compagnies (Vertical Booking, Djubo and NextPax) for their simple tool providing recommendations and actions to improve vacation rental content quality/score

30% Creativity & Innovation
